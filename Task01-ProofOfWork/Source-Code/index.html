Our Story

Founded with Vision,
Driven by People

Established in 2020 by Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation started as a small group of dreamers in Bilaspur and has grown into a nationwide movement for social justice.

Our work is built on the pillars of transparency and scalability, ensuring that every rupee donated goes directly to the field where it's needed most. We believe that lasting change only comes from within communities.
